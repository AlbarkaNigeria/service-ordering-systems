Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?
We deliver fresh food every day from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Any orders placed after 9:30 PM will be scheduled for delivery the following morning.
How long does delivery usually take?
Standard delivery takes between 30 to 45 minutes depending on your exact location and current traffic conditions. We always strive to get your food to you while it's hot!
Can I change or cancel my order?
Yes, but please be quick! You can modify or cancel your order by sending us a message on WhatsApp within 5 minutes of placing it. Once the kitchen starts preparing your meal, we cannot accept cancellations.
Do you offer bulk orders for events?
Absolutely! We cater to birthdays, office parties, and family gatherings. Please send us a WhatsApp message at least 24 hours in advance to discuss your menu and get a special discount.
How do I pay for my food?
You can pay via Bank Transfer to our official account before delivery, or you can pay via POS/Cash when our delivery rider arrives at your doorstep.
